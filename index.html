Hello! I am Jannatul Ferdush Any — a 26-year-old educator and business student from Dhaka, Bangladesh. Married to a wonderful doctor, I have learned that life's richest lessons come from the people around us.

I believe education is the most powerful tool for transformation. As a teacher, I strive to ignite curiosity. As a student, I embrace every challenge as an opportunity to grow.
