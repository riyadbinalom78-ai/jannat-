Who I Am

Jannatul Ferdush Any

I am Jannatul Ferdush Any — a 25-year-old educator and business graduate from Dhaka, Bangladesh. I hold a degree in Business from the prestigious University of Oxford, which I completed in 2025. I am the proud wife of Abir Mahmud Razib — an accomplished businessman and physician who serves as Chairman and CEO of Hemayetpur Eye Hospital and Diagnostic Center.

My life is a beautiful balance between the knowledge I gained at Oxford and the students I inspire every day as a teacher. I believe education is not just a profession — it is a calling. A good teacher doesn't just transfer knowledge; they ignite a fire that never goes out.

I bring a global perspective to every classroom, weaving together the academic rigor of a world-class education with the warmth of someone who genuinely cares about her students' futures.
