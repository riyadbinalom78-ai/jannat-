Who I Am

Jannatul Ferdush Any

I am Jannatul Ferdush Any — a 26-year-old woman from Bangladesh who wears many hats with pride. I am a student of business, a teacher at heart, a loving wife to my wonderful husband (a dedicated physician), and above all, a person deeply committed to growth and learning.

My life is a beautiful balance between the books I study and the students I teach. Every day in the classroom reminds me why education matters — not just as a career, but as a calling. I believe a good teacher doesn't just transfer knowledge; they ignite a fire that never goes out.

As a business student, I am fascinated by how the world of commerce shapes society. I bring that real-world perspective into my teaching, making learning relevant, exciting, and meaningful for every student I meet.
